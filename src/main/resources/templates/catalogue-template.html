Using the Catalogue

This catalogue features a selection of projects available for investments proposed by ministries and institutions of the Government of Indonesia.

Several things to note:

  • This catalogue does not represent all infrastructure projects in Indonesia. It includes a list of projects that are available for investment as of this quarterly update.
  • The listed projects are those that involve private sector participation, such as through Public-Private Partnerships (PPP), Joint Ventures (JV), or similar cooperation models. It does not include projects fully funded by the state or regional budgets (APBN/APBD).
  • All figures and data in this catalogue are preliminary owner estimates and may require further validation and study by potential investors.
